## Overview

Frontbuffer ("we", "us", or "our") operates frontbuffer.net. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information.

## Information We Collect

We do not directly collect personal information from visitors. However, third-party services we use may collect data as described below.

## Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics (GA4) to understand how visitors interact with our site. Google Analytics may collect pages visited and time spent, general geographic location, device and browser type, and referring websites. Data is anonymized and does not personally identify individual users. You can opt out via [Google Analytics Opt-out](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout).

## Google AdSense

We may display advertisements served by Google AdSense. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. You can opt out at [Google Ads Settings](https://www.google.com/settings/ads).

## Cookies

Our site uses cookies through Google Analytics and Google AdSense. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Articles on Frontbuffer may contain links to external sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites.

## Children's Privacy

Frontbuffer does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.
