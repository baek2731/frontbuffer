**Frontbuffer** is an independent tech and gaming analysis publication.

We cover the hardware, software, and industry stories that actually matter — with research-backed reporting and no hype.

Every article goes through a multi-stage editorial process: topic research, source verification, and fact-checking before publication.

---

### Editorial Standards

- All specific claims are sourced and cited
- No affiliate bias in editorial coverage
- AI-assisted drafting with human editorial review and fact-checking

### Disclosure

This site uses AI tools as part of its editorial workflow. All content is reviewed, fact-checked, and edited by human editors before publication. For details, see our [Disclosure](/disclosure/) page.
